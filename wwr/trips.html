Our River Adventures

Half-Day Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families, this 3-hour guided rafting trip navigates exciting Class II-III rapids. Participants receive a full safety briefing, helmets, life jackets, and paddles.

Equipment Included: Helmet, life jacket, paddle, safety orientation.

Full-Day Expedition

A full-day adventure through scenic river canyons featuring thrilling Class III-IV rapids. This trip includes a riverside lunch, advanced paddling instruction, and breathtaking natural views.

Equipment Included: All rafting gear, lunch, dry bags, safety team.

Extreme Rapids Experience

Designed for thrill-seekers, this advanced trip tackles powerful Class IV-V rapids. Participants must have prior rafting experience. Our expert guides lead this adventure with strict safety protocols.

Equipment Included: Safety gear, helmets, life jackets, guide team.
